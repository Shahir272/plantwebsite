Kiwi Fruit

The Fuzzy Vitamin Bomb

Planting Steps

01

Male & Female Plants

Most kiwi varieties require both a male and a female plant for pollination. Typically, plant one male for every 8 female vines.

02

Trellis Construction

Kiwi vines are vigorous and heavy. Construct a strong T-bar trellis or pergola system to support the weight of the mature vine.

03

Planting

Plant in spring after frost risk. Space plants 10-15 feet apart. Ensure soil is slightly acidic and well-draining.

04

Heavy Watering

Kiwis are thirsty plants. Mulch heavily to retain moisture and water frequently during the growing season.
