Dragon Fruit

The Exotic Cactus Vine

Planting Steps

01

Prepare the Support

Dragon fruit is a heavy climbing cactus. Before planting, build a sturdy wooden post or concrete pillar (approx 5-6ft tall) with a strong frame at the top.

02

Soil Mix & Planting

Use a well-draining sandy mix. Combine potting soil, sand, and perlite. Plant the cutting near the post and gently tie the stem to it as it grows.

03

Pruning & Care

Prune side branches until the stem reaches the top of the trellis. Then, let it branch out like an umbrella. This induces flowering.

04

Pollination

Many varieties are self-sterile and bloom at night. You may need to hand-pollinate the large white flowers using a clean brush.
