Lemon Tree

Sunshine in Fruit Form

Planting Steps

01

Pot vs Ground

Lemons love heat. If you live in a cold zone, plant in a large pot on wheels so you can move it indoors during winter.

02

Soil Requirements

Citrus hates "wet feet". Use a specialized citrus mix or amend your soil with plenty of sand and compost to ensure sharp drainage.

03

Feeding

Lemons are hungry plants. Feed with a nitrogen-rich citrus fertilizer 3-4 times a year, especially in early spring and summer.

04

Pest Watch

Watch out for leaf miners and aphids. Use neem oil spray as a preventative measure to keep leaves glossy and healthy.
