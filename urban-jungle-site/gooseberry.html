Gooseberry

The Tart Treat

Planting Steps

01

Site Selection

Gooseberries prefer cooler spots. Morning sun and afternoon shade is ideal in warmer climates to prevent leaf scorch.

02

Air Circulation

They are prone to mildew. Plant bushes 5 feet apart to ensure good airflow. Select resistant varieties like 'Hinnonmakis'.

03

Pruning

Prune in winter to create an open "goblet" shape. This makes harvesting easier (avoiding the thorns!) and improves fruit size.

04

Harvesting

Pick green for cooking (pies/jams) in June, or wait until they turn soft and sweet (red/yellow) in July for eating fresh.
