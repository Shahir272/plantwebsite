Passion Fruit

The Tropical Flavor Bomb

Planting Steps

01

Choose a Sunny Spot

Passion fruit vines thrive in full sun. They need at least 6-8 hours of direct light daily to produce sweetness.

02

Prepare the Soil

These vines are heavy feeders. Enrich the soil with plenty of compost and chicken manure before planting.

03

Support System

A chain-link fence or a strong wire trellis is ideal. The vine uses tendrils to grip and climb rapidly.

04

Harvest

Don't pick the fruit! Wait for it to fall to the ground. That is when it is perfectly ripe and sweetest.
